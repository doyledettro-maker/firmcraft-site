## Scoring Methodology

### Firmcraft Priority Score (1-5)

Each company is scored on fit for Firmcraft's AI phone answering and communication platform:

| Score | Label | Criteria |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **5** | Critical | Clear communication pain signals (missed calls, bad responsiveness reviews), no online booking, broken/dated website, medium-sized company with revenue to spend |
| **4** | High | Some communication gaps, limited digital presence, no online booking, established business with growth potential |
| **3** | Medium | Decent business but no urgent pain signals; may benefit from Firmcraft but lower urgency |
| **2** | Low-Medium | Already has good digital presence, online booking, or too small/new to prioritize |
| **1** | Low | Too large (in-house call center), ethical concerns, or possibly winding down |
| **0** | Excluded | Not HVAC, acquired, or otherwise not a valid prospect |

### Website Quality Score (1-5)

| Score | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **5** | Excellent — professional design, fast, mobile-optimized, online booking, strong SEO |
| **4** | Professional — clean design, good functionality, may have online booking |
| **3** | Decent — functional but using templates, room for improvement |
| **2** | Basic — dated design, template sites, HTTP-only, poor UX |
| **1** | Broken/Missing — domain down, for sale, placeholder content, or non-functional |
