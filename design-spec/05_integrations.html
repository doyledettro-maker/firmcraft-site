Your weird tool

Custom connector · week 1

When the list isn't long enough

If it has an API, a portal, or a webpage — we can drive it.

About a third of our customers' workflows hit a tool that's not in this catalog. A regional carrier's claim portal. An in-house ERP your last consultant built. A government filing site that's older than half the team. None of it stops us.

Three ways the operator can speak to a tool: a documented API (preferred), an undocumented but reachable HTTP endpoint, or a browser session driving the actual UI when there's no programmatic surface at all. Most "weird tool" connectors take 1–3 days.
